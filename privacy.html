Shree Finance Services - Privacy Policy

Who is Covered by this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors ("Visitors") who access the website http://wwceservices.org and provide information to Shree Finance Services online.

Information Covered by this Policy

This Policy covers the following information collected from Visitors:

  • Private information provided to Shree Finance Services online.
  • Information collected by the bank server from the visitor's browser ("the Information").

Use of Information

Shree Finance Services collects information for the following purposes:

  • To provide Visitors with the best possible services.
  • To enable Shree Finance Services to provide services, compile transactions, perform credit reporting, or comply with banking regulations and terms agreed upon by the Visitor.
  • To share information with external organizations only when necessary for the aforementioned purposes.
  • To provide Visitors with superior services and offers.
  • To improve the visitor experience on the website and recommend products of interest.

Cookies

The Shree Finance Services website uses cookies. Cookies are small data files stored on your computer. Shree Finance Services uses two types of cookies:

  • Persistent Cookies: Store non-personal information (browser, ISP, OS, clickstream data) and profiling information (age, gender, income) without storing personal details like name, account number, or contact information.
  • Future Use: Shree Finance Services may implement cookie encryption in the future.

    • Shree Finance Services uses cookie information to:

    • Improve visitor experience by displaying relevant content.
    • Store visitor preferences for easier navigation.

    Shree Finance Services may also disclose information as permitted or required by law.

    Personal Information Collected

    Shree Finance Services may collect the following personal information for service purposes:

    • Identification Information: Name, gender, address, phone number, date of birth, marital status, email address.
    • PAN, KYC Status, Signature, and Photograph.
    • Other details: Any information required for service provision.

    Consent Withdrawal

    You can withdraw your consent to process your personal data by emailing care@shreefinanceservices.org using your registered email ID. Shree Finance Services will process your request within 10 business days. However, data retention may be necessary for legal and regulatory compliance. Withdrawing consent may affect certain services or functionalities.
